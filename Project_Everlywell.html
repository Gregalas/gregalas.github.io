Everlywell

Transforming the future of digital healthcare

What was the project

The reality for many of us is that digital healthcare is now the norm and people's expecations for high quality online health and wellness services are higher than ever. My team at Metalab were tasked with helping Everlywell sharpen their edge and build their moat as a startup within a highly competitive category.

Over 6 months we carved out a new guiding vision for Everywell’s digital platforms, taking shape through a redesigned mobile app, core e-commerce experience, as well as an evolutionary brand strategy to support broader product offerings and sub-brand connectivity.

What I did

I played a key role through all stages of discovery and the divergent phases of design. I collaborated with research and brand teams to shape a compelling narrative for their product vision. Additionally, I was a key member of the team that redesigned the entire e-commerce platform and designed the framework for healthcare programs.

The Impact

In the end the work we delivered created massive impact for Everylwell. Their e-commerce website saw a 35% increase in new members for their wellness programs, and more than 21% increase in conversion for their checkout flows. We also saw a 53% increase in conversion for their membership page product purchases.
