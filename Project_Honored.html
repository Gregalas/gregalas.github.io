HonorEd

Reimagining online education

What was the project

Imagine a world where all people have access to an advanced education of the highest standards no matter where they are in world. An education that allows the flexibility to work from anywhere, while still fostering creative and critical thinking and a sense of community.

This was a 6 month project where our goal was to help Honor Eduction bring that world to life by designing the vision for their product as well as an MVP to help with their initial launch.

What I did

Coming into the project a few months after it kicked off, my role was to help shape core parts of the user experience, such as navigation, fundamentals of the different learning environments, designing novel interactive patterns to help students better engage with their lecturers and learning material, as well as refining the visual direction and putting together the design system.

Design Systems

To empower students through better time management:

We designed a flexible class and module planner to help students manage their time and lives on their own terms. The planner encourages students to take control of their schedules and learn time management skills. It allows them to shift classes, provides session recordings, and suggests recommended study times.

To help students stay connected with eachother and course material:

We designed an activity feed to work in unison with the planner. It functions as a conventional to-do list by highlighting specific items within the timeline that are coming up or are due next. The intent is to nudge positive behaviour while avoiding a sense of “being told what to do”. It helps create a connection between your actions and workload, and gives you insights to help improve time management.

To improve engagement and content discovery:

We built tools that help student's react to content using fun and delightful interactions. For example, by triggerring the radial reaction menu you can quickly tag a moment during a lecture using universal labels that help you find that content later and enhance content application for lecturers.

Impact and key takeaways.

The project went on to be a huge success for Metalab and HonorEd as we provided their team with a firm foundation to build towards a successful launch. This was a really high pressure project with high stakes and a few massive personalities for our team to manage and impress.

On personal level their was a ton of growth in learning to work under extremely tight deadlines while still producing high quality work, as well as being comfortable regularly presenting back ideas and solutions to a boardroom of some of the industries most experienced product personalities.
