HonorEd

Reimagining online education

What was the project

Imagine a world where all people have access to an advanced education of the highest standards no matter where they are in world... An education that allows the flexiblity to work from anywhere, while still fostering creative and critical thinking and a sense of community.

Over a 6 month period our team at MetaLab helped Honor Eduction envision that world. I jumped in at a critical stage of the project to work on key features that would help realise their mission of democratizing university level education.

What I did

As senior PD on the project, I collaborated closely with the design, research and product teams to formulate a product strategy and roadmap for us to build out over a 12-month period, alongside the MetaLab and Exiger engineering teams. I was lucky enough to work end-to-end across all stages of the product design process and played a lead role in several key phases, specifically around product strategy, UX and UI design, as well as building out Exiger's new design system

Design Systems

Empowering students through better time management

We designed a flexible class and module planner to help students manage their time and lives on their own terms. The planner encourages students to take control of their schedules and learn time management skills. It allows them to shift classes, provides session recordings, and suggests recommended study times.

An activity feed that keeps students connected

The feed is designed to work in unison with the planner. It functions as a conventional To-do list by highlighting specific items within the timeline that are coming up or are due next. The intent is to nudge positive behaviour while avoiding a sense of ‚Äúbeing told what to do‚Äù. It helps create a connection between your actions and workload, and how you can best manage it.
