Acapela

Better hybrid collaboration, fewer meetings, more time for you.

The Mission

Do meetings suck, or do we just suck at running them? It’s a critical question that dives deep into the social dynamics and status quo of how we've been operating in the modern workplace.

This was an end-to-end project where we designed an MVP that transforms meetings into collaborative workspaces, empowering teams to resolve meetings before they start. Beyond protecting people's time, these spaces were designed to boost team connectivity and morale, despite the intention being asynchronous-first.

What I did

UX Design, Product Strategy, Visual Design, Design Systems

Protecting people’s time, with topics

In order to maximize productivity and efficiency, we introduced the concept of a 'topic' to facilitate pre-meeting discussions. A topic represents an agenda item that requires action, either before the meeting begins or synchronously if it remains unresolved. By ensuring sufficient resolution of topics, we automatically remove the corresponding meeting from your calendar.

Fostering connection and prioritizing time

By default, spaces and rooms are designed to be open and accessible. If a meeting is scheduled on the calendar, you're encouraged to explore the AI-generated meeting notes, quickly review the key discussion points, and even contribute if you find it necessary.

The result

In the end we left the client buzzing with anticipation as they held a complete vision MVP ready to start implementing into their roadmap. This project turned out to be one of the most fulfilling collaborative experiences I've ever had!
