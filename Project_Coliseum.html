Coliseum

Designing a new vision for motorsport fan engagement.

Can we save motorsport and build a new frontier for fan engagement? When I heard about this client and brief I was stoked to say the least. Primo, I am a massive sports fan, and think Formula 1 is awesome… Secondo, these types of "blue sky" projects are incredibly fun as I really get to push my imagination without any real limitations.

Our mission:

Help coliseum design a vision for how to better engage motorsport fans and grow the sport organically, starting with fans in the USA.

Start your engines

Racing into research

At MetaLab, we usually start projects like this with a round of Tarantino's, which is basically where we go wild imagining what the five-star experience of a product looks like, no bars held.

Based on the hypothesis that to maximize engagement and generate consistent interest we needed to go beyond race events themselves. We landed on a strategy to design a social audio platform focused on ongoing motorsport conversation. Our research played a crucial role in the project, providing valuable insights that propelled us forward in the design phase:

  • Clear barriers to entry still exist within motorsports.
  • Motorsport content does not adapt to fans' learning styles or knowledge levels.
  • The current fan experience facilitates passive spectatorship as opposed to active participation.
  • Building psychological safety in digital spaces is a challenge, particularly as communities scale.

    • Building community through social audio

    Once we’d confirmed the viability, potential for impact and capacity to revitalize the motor sports community, we started to notice key problems we needed to address that were common across the social audio landscape within products like Spotify Live, Twitch, and Clubhouse.

    Levelling the playing field

    We noticed that some people love to just listen in, and don't have the desire to contribute in the same way others do. Also, discussions are often dominated by certain people, and that affects overall participation. To address this, we designed different ways of empowering listeners to participate in conversations that made them feel comfortable, and cultivated a more democratic listening environment. This had both user and business value by means of increasing retention rate.

    Giving everyone a voice

    To make live audio rooms more fair and to encourage equal participation, we designed a feature that allows you to submit and vote on topics that matter to you, which are then discussed alongside your favorite motorsport heroes. Stream previews and topics empower people with more freedom of choice about what content they commit to before diving in fully. Moreover, we created a range of basic reactions that enable emotional connection without the need for direct communication in the channel.

    Driving impact

    Our solution invloved a unique streaming experience that set Coliseum apart from its competitors. It's immersive, and interactive, and allows people to engage with their motorsport heoroes, such as Max Verstappen and Louis Hamilton, as well as other fans in a way that makes them feel like they're not just another voice in the crowd.
