Coliseum

Designing a new vision for motorsport fan engagement.

The Mission

Save motorsport and build a new frontier for fan engagement! When I heard about this client and brief I was stoked to say the least. Primo, I am a massive sports fan, and think Formula 1 is awesome… Secondo, these types of "blue sky" projects are incredibly fun as I really get to push my imagination without any real limitations. Our big goal was to help coliseum design a vision for how to better engage motorsport fans and grow the sport organically, starting with fans in the USA.

What I did

Product Strategy, Visual design, User Experience Design and Research Collaboration

Levelling the playing field

To improve retention and enhance engagement we designed a set of features that empowers all listeners in audio rooms, and creates a more democratic social environment.

Impact

Our solution invloved a unique streaming experience that set Coliseum apart from its competitors. It's immersive, and interactive, and allows people to engage with their motorsport heoroes, such as Max Verstappen and Louis Hamilton, as well as other fans in a way that makes them feel like they're not just another voice in the crowd.
