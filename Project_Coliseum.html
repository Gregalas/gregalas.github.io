Coliseum

Talk motorsport, together with the best racing personalities.

Can we save motorsport? Our client team came to MetaLab looking for answers. Our mission was to help Coliseum design a vision for how to better engage motorsport fans and grow the sport organically within the USA. The business opportunity lay in tapping into and growing the younger generation of motorsport fans by renewing their connection to the sport on their own playing field – social media.

I was stoked to say the least. Primo, I love sport, and and think Formula 1 is awesome! Secondo, these types of "blue sky" projects are usually increldibly fun as I really get to push my imagination and am not confined to any real technical limitations of a creating production ready designs.

Talk Motorsport, 24-7

As with most projects like this, we started with a round of tarantino's. Which is basically where we go wild imaging a five-star experience for the product, no bars held. Based on the hypothesis that to maximise engagement and generate consistent interest, race conversations should be ongoing rather than limited to race events. We eventually landed on a strategy to design a social audio platform focused on ongoing motorsport conversation.

Our research – one of the most exciting and engaging phases of the project – fueled us with insights that truly helped propeled us forward into design:

  • Clear barriers to entry still exist within motorsports.
  • Motorsport content does not adapt to fans' learning styles or knowledge levels.
  • The current fan experience facilitates passive spectatorship as opposed to active participation.
  • Building psychological safety in digital spaces is a challenge, particularly as communities scale.

    • Levelling the playing field.

    When it comes to audio rooms, we picked up that some people love to just listen in, and don't have the desire to contribute in the same way others do. Also, discussions are often dominated by certain people, and that affects overall participation. So we designed different ways of empowering listeners to participate in the conversation in ways that made them comfortable and creating a more democratic listening environment. This had both user and business value by means of increasing retention rate.
    To make live audio rooms more fair and to encourage equal participation, we designed a feature that allows you to submit and vote on topics that matter to you, which are then discussed alongside your favorite motorsport heroes. Stream previews and topics empower people with more freedom of choice about what content they commit to before diving in fully. Moreover, we created a range of basic reactions that enable emotional connection without the need for direct communication in the channel.

    The finish line

    Our solution invloved a unique streaming experience that set Coliseum apart from its competitors. It's immersive, and interactive, and allows people to engage with their motorsport heoroes, such as Max Verstappen and Louis Hamilton, as well as other fans in a way that makes them feel like they're not just another voice in the crowd.

    In the end, working closely with Eilís (our researcher) to develop and test concepts was a dream, and I had a blast collaborating with Nuno (my PD counterpart) in design, working with Cam (Design lead) to bring our strategic vision to life. To the best of my knowledge, the designs have yet to be built, but I'm eager to see if this takes off in the future!
