Hey, I'm Greg

I'm a Senior Product Designer at MetaLab, where my focus has largely been around end-to-end design for early stage startups from proof of concept to launch, and evolving product for large enterprise level organisations.

Previously I was a Lead Product Designer at a startup called 22seven and have worked at several other award-winning digital design agencies.

First, philosophy

These principles provide a snapshot of how I like to think about leadership, product, and design and lean on these whenever I can when making decicions.
  1. Find the real problem before solving it. More important than any design or solution is the accuracy in a diagnosis.
  2. Build products that are real, and beautiful. Make things that people can actually use and enjoy, no matter how small the project is. Keep looking for chances to make a difference.
  3. Be fiercely curious. You'll never have to motivate yourself if you stay curious about the world. Stay hungry and eager to learn.
  4. Promote objectivity and find the truth. Focus your energy on getting things right, as opposed to being right.
  5. Put others on. Not eveyone's had the same chances you've had in life. Share your knowledge, teach skills, pay it forward.

Then craft

I love to create simple solutions to problems other people seem to overlook and that really need to be solved. My approach involves delivering solutions informally, beginning with the simplest version and then iterating rapidly.
