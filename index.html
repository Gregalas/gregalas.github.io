Manifesto

I believe everyone should do work that gives their life meaning. I also believe we all need a manifesto. Here's mine:

  1. Designing with soulCrafting products that feel human, not just functional. Building software with personality, that connects emotionally.
  2. Aiming for remarkablePrioritising great experiences and solving problems. Function before form, always.
  3. Leading through design Helping others grow through the work itself. Leading with kindness, patience and compassion.
  4. Growing over achieving Getting my dopamine from improving things, innovating and learning, as opposed to being validated.
