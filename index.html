HI, MY NAME IS GREG

I'm a Senior Product Designer at MetaLab, where my focus has largely been around building and scaling early stage startups from proof of concept to launch, executing at all stages of the product design process.

Previously I was a Lead Product Designer at a startup called 22seven and have worked at several other award-winning digital design agencies.

FIRST, PRINCIPLES

These principles provide a snapshot of how I like to think about leadership, product, and design and lean on these whenever I can.
  1. Go beyond problem solving. Push products to be the best version of themselves. Never settle for mediocre MVP's.
  2. Eliminate suffering and increase happiness Design software that is meaningful, and which in some way increases the happiness, or reduces the suffering of others.
  3. Trust your voice and speak with passion. Your voice is a design tool. Use it to motivate teams, and accelerate product toward being beautiful and useful.
  4. Stay curious. Be part of teams that that are innovative, curious and independent-minded. These are the teams that will always drive innovation, problem-solving, and strive for continuous improvement.
  5. “With great power comes great responsibility”. - Uncle Ben Design products and build teams that promote objectivity and truth. Strive to eliminate bias, and use technology to help humans, for good!

INTO THE WORK

I am T-shaped. My inclination is to attack a problem by constructing a narrative and iteratively build a mosaic of potential solutions. I believe this requires a broad understanding of various expertise and systems. So I'm continuously learning about design, UX, business, product thinking, coding, leadership, and the other million things there are to learn in the world of product design.
