An unusual

Product designer

I'm a human who makes life better through design. I’ve designed for startups, big brands, and everything in between. I’m currently a senior product designer at Metalab.

First, philosophy

My career has always been about doing what's meaningful rather than what's easy. Here’s what gives my work meaning:

  1. Empowering peopleSolving meaningful problems in the world through iterative design and compassion.
  2. Striving for clarity Designing products that are simple and easy to use. Being intentional about every design decision.
  3. Standing up for good Combating harmful practices in tech. Protecting people’s wellbeing and safety online.
  4. Designing without fear Using established patterns where ever possible but never being afraid to innovate and push boundaries
  5. Clearing the path for others Being insatiably curious about people and giving them tools to unlock and strengthen their superpowers.
  6. Growing over achieving Getting my dopamine from improving things, innovating and learning, as opposed to being validated.

Then craft

I’m a weird cube shaped designer – constantly learning everything I can about product and design. I'm all about influencing product strategy through execution of a design vision, and designing beautifully crafted digital products that customers love to use.
