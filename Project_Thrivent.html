Thrivent

Smart banking that gives you purpose.

What was the project

Thrivent, a Fortune 500 company who has roots in insurance and charity had it's sights on preparing for the future of their business. They wanted to start progressing toward the banking sector and asked our team at MetaLab to help them design an MVP product to help inspire a younger generation of customers to start using their bank and services.

Our focus was to wow Thrivent's board of directors and ensure another round of business so we could go on and actually build our designs in the coming months. We succeeded.

What I did

Helped design the end-to-end experience for Thrivent’s MVP, with a core focus on UX and visual design. Owned key parts of the product, such as the customer support and the design system. Played a fundamental role in translating the new brand into product.

The Impact

Apart from getting to nerd out on American fintech – which I really do enjoy for some odd reason – it was super inspiring to be part of building a financial product that has an alternative view of money. One which emphasises the social and societal impact of money as tools for achieving social goals, reducing economic disparities and helping people to become autonomous money managers.

The outcome of it all was an impressed client which won MetaLab a second stream of work where these concepts were brought to life even more.
