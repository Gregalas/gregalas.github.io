Thrivent

Smart banking that gives you purpose.

The mission

Thrivent, a Fortune 500 company who has roots in insurance and charity, wanted to start progressing toward the banking sector and asked MetaLab to help build a product to talked to a younger, and upcoming generation of customers.

While traditional banks, and even the so called "disruptors", focus on making money for their shareholders, our focus was to help Thrivent on their mission to create a bank that is purpose-based and advice driven. A bank that provides resources and services in a way that puts people ahead of profits.

Humanising support

A critical insight from our research was how frustrated people are with banking support systems. People wanted to be able to meet with a human in a way that reflects their lived experience. For example, people prefer to opt-in to choose an advisor themselves and we found that specialisation, languages spoken, and availability were important characteristics when it came to making a choice.

The solution was an omnichannel support experience that helps you find an an advisor that matches your specific financial needs so you can feel good about the advice you get. You can browse each advisor’s bio to get a sense of their personality and expertise before making your choice, leaving you feeling confident and more supported your discussion.

Measuring financial clarity

We used visual metaphors of fluidity and colour spectrums within in our data visualisation for net worth to illustrate the idea that your wealth is fluid, normalising the inevitable fluctuation of financial growth but while always encouraging a plan as the superordinate goal.

Impact

From a personal perspective, it was really inspiring to be part of building a financial product that has an alternative view of money, one which emphasises the social and societal impact of money as tools for achieving social goals, reducing economic disparities and helping people to become autonomous money managers.
