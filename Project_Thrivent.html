Thrivent

Smart banking that gives you purpose.

What was the project

Thrivent is a Fortune 500 company who has roots in insurance and charity. Their goal is to start future proofing their business by progressing toward the banking sector and asked our team at Metalab to help them design an MVP product to help inspire a younger generation of customers to start using their services.

What I did

As part of a 3-person design team which included a brand designer, my focus was to translate our stakeholder’s vision into the end-to-end experience, with a specific focus on features such as customer support and accounts, while also playing a key role in brand-to-product translation and building out our UI Kit.

UX Design

The Impact

Apart from the incredible learning experience and getting to nerd out on fintech, it was inspiring to be part of building a financial product that has an alternative view of money, and to help Thrivent achieve their goal of reducing economic disparities and helping people to become autonomous money managers.

The outcome of it all was an impressed client and a second stream of business for Metalab.
