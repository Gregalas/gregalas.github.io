About me

Timeline and milestones:

I grew up in Johannesburg, South Africa, the world's largest urban forest.

In 2008, I graduated from The Greenside Design Centre with a bachelor's degree in Multimedia Design and completed an honors degree in Visual Communications from Vega in 2009.

In 2009, after an internship an JWT, I landed my first job as Junior Digital Designer at a small agency called Creative Solutions, and then joined a digital product and marketing agency called Clarity, working on brands such as Virgin Mobile and Vodacom.

In 2010 I joined Gloo Design, where I helped design digital products for BMW, MINI, First National Bank, and South African Tourism.

In 2013, I took a leap of faith by going back to university to study Biokinetics, with an undergraduate degree in Sports Psychology. I continued to freelance as a designer while at Universtity.

In 2015, I conducted clinical research at The University of Cape Town and wrote a paper comparing the injury risks between trail and road runners.

In 2019, I joined a fintech startup called 22seven where I helped ship loads of features in their iOS, Android, and Web apps.

In 2020 I was promoted to lead designer at 22seven, part of 5 strong design team that shipped the refactored version of the iOS app.

Since 2021, I have been helping build and accelerate products for early-to-mid-stage startups and enterprise-level organizations at MetaLab.
