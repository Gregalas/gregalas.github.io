As a kid growing up in Johannesburg South Africa, I became passionate about design through immersion in graffiti art and skateboarding culture. Despite always knowing that I wanted to be a designer, I was too curious to follow a traditional career path. This led me to explore different occupations and ways of thinking about the world. Although my CV reflects my experimental approach, design has been a constant thread throughout my career.
