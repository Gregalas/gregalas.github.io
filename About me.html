About me

A brief overview of how my careers progressed.

Timeline and milestones:

I grew up in Johannesburg, South Africa, the world's largest urban forest! It's where my passion for digital design and graffiti first took root. I started tinkering with MS Paint at the age of 7, and before long, I was creating character designs in Maya, and busting murals all over the city with “The Cosmic Crew”.

In 2008/09, I graduated from The Greenside Design Centre with a bachelor's degree in Multimedia Design and went on to complete an honors degree in Visual Communications from Vega.

Soon after, I landed my first web design job at a small agency called Creative Solutions, where I built flash and HTML websites for property companies in the city. By the end of 2009, I found myself working for Clarity, a digital product, and marketing agency. There I worked on brands such as Virgin Mobile and Vodacom.

In 2011, I joined the award-winning team at Gloo Design, where I started to mature my skills as a digital designer, working on blue-chip brands such as BMW, MINI, First National Bank, and South African Tourism.

In 2013, I was like, "You know what? I gotta scratch this itch I've got for sports medicine!" And I ended up heading back to university to dive deeper into the field. I continued to freelance as a designer while studying to become a Biokineticist at the University of Johannesburg and The University of Cape Town. This was a combined degree that involved an undergraduate in Sports Psychology.

From 2013 to 2016, I gained a thorough understanding of research methods, how to work with data, and eventually, strong patient management skills. All of which became extremely useful in my work as a product designer.

In 2016, I qualified with a BSc (Med)(Hons) Biokinetics and began practicing as a clinician while still taking on freelance jobs as a designer.

In 2019, I took a full-time position at a fintech startup with a money management app called 22seven, where I learned a ton about fintech and personal finance, and helped ship loads of cool features accross their iOS, Android and Web apps.

In 2020, I was promoted to a lead designer at 22seven and helped lead a team of 2 UX designers and 1 marketing designer.

In 2021 I started working at MetaLab where I have been helping build and accelerate products for early-to-mid-stage startups and enterprise-level organizations within ed-tech, esports, productivity and collaboration, dating, social, fintech, and risk management.

I currently live with my partner in the beautiful city of Cape Town, South Africa.
