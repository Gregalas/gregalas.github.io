Exiger

Making the world a safer place for businesses to succeed.

What was the project

Exiger helps businesses, government agencies and banks navigate risk and compliance for their third parties, supply chains and individual customers. Over the years, they had developed several tools aimed at assisting different customer segments in different ways. My team at Metalab were tasked to build an MVP and product vision that consolidates these tools into one powerful tool named OneExiger, allowing them to scale their existing offerings, all while providing a best-in-class user experience that would distinguish them from competitors in their category.

What I did

I played a key role across all stages of the design process from inception until launch, by helping develop the product strategy, user experience and visual direction. In addition, I lead the design system build and collaborated closely with product and engineering teams to help ensure a successful launch of the product.

The Impact

From brand, to research, to reinventing the platform from the ground up and building out their design system, we did it all. This was a years worth of work where our team touched almost every product offerring, culminating in the public release of OneExiger in July 2023. Since the release we've had feedback from Exiger customers saying this is 'one of the most innovative products they've ever used. Exiger has since been named one of Fast Companies Brand's That Matter.
