Z LEAGUE

The world's first skill-based tournament platform casual gamers.

We built helped our client, Z League, build the world's first skill-based tournament platform because some amateur gamers like watching streamers and pros compete in tournaments, but there were no good options to compete themselves. With Z League, everyone can compete in fair, fun and meaningful competitions.

My team and I at MetaLab had a blast redefining their product and building Z League's MVP to release it market in just 7 short months, while establishing a solid runway for their team to elevate their product with features like a customisable profile and teams which are now live in their mobile app on iOS.
