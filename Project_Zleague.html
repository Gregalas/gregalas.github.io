Z League

The world's first skill-based tournament platform for casual gamers.

What was the project

Z League exists because amateur gamers were getting a raw deal when it came to competing for cash in online tournaments. They provide a platform for gamers to compete in fair tournaments and win cash prizes. The problem was they had a great product but a poor online experience and no mobile app – the main touch point gamers wanted to sign up for and track their progress in tournaments.

Our mission was to help Z League design an iOS app that allows gamers to join and track their progress in tournaments while providing a first class experience for connecting with their friends and playing online.

What I did

I played a key role in all stages of the design process by helping design the core product experience for the MVP and product vision, helping develop the visual direction, and build out their design system.
Product Strategy
UX Design
Visual Design
Interaction Design
Design Systems

The Impact

Within 6 months, we launched the Z League app to an eager user base setting their business up for hyper-growth and increased revenue by improving tournament registration and player retention.
