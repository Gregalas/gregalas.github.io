Z League

The world's first skill-based tournament platform for casual gamers.

What was the project

The big problem was that amateur gamers could easily watch streamers and pros compete, but there were no good options to compete themselves, at least fairly and for higher stakes.

In steps MetaLab! Our goal was to help redefine Z League's product in a way that improves the overall experience for players, and increases tournament registration and retention so players keep coming back for more. Within 4 months we designed, built and released their first ever mobile application to their global user base.

What I did

I played a key role accross all stages of the design process helping set up the core product experience for future hyper-growth and revenue.
Product Strategy
UX Design
Visual Design
Interaction Design
Design Systems

The Impact

Within 6 months, we redesigned the Z League app for mobile and released it to an eager user base.

Importantly, we created business impact by helping shape Z League's future roadmap with exciting features that will ensure they’ll remain competitive within their category, and continue providing value and enjoyment to their users.
